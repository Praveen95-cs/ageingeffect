Aging Side Effects

  • Wrinkles: A common side effect of aging as the skin loses elasticity.
  • Cognitive Decline: Aging may slow down thinking processes and affect memory.
  • Bone Density Loss: This increases the risk of fractures and osteoporosis.
  • Muscle Loss: Muscle mass decreases with age, affecting strength and mobility.
